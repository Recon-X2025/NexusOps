# Strategy & Projects

The Strategy Center is the home for portfolio and project work. It lives under
**Strategy Center** in the sidebar, with the PMO workbench as the operational entry
point.

## Strategy Center

The overview (`/app/strategy`) gives leadership a portfolio-level view of initiatives
and their health, tying execution back to strategic objectives and OKRs.

## Initiatives & projects

Initiatives and projects (`/app/projects`) are the unit of delivery, each with a
detail page (`/app/projects/[id]`) covering scope, milestones, tasks, and status.
Projects connect to the OKRs defined in Performance and HR so that delivery progress
rolls up to objectives.

## PMO workbench

The PMO workbench (`/app/workbench/pmo`) is a concentrated workspace for project
managers — a single place to triage projects, track milestones, and manage delivery
across the portfolio.
