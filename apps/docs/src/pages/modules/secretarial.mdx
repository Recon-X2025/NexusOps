# Legal & Governance (Company Secretary)

The Secretarial module supports India company-secretarial work: board meetings and
resolutions, MCA/ROC filings, share capital and ESOP, statutory registers, and a
compliance calendar. It lives under **Legal & Governance → Secretarial**
(`/app/secretarial`) with tabs for Company Overview, Board & Meetings, MCA/ROC
Filings, Share Capital & ESOP, Statutory Registers, and Compliance Calendar.

## Board meetings

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Scheduled** | Meeting set but not yet held. |
| **In progress** | Meeting under way. |
| **Completed** | Held; minutes finalised. |
| **Cancelled** | Meeting cancelled. |
| **Adjourned** | Paused, to resume later. |

Meeting types include board meeting, audit committee, nomination committee,
compensation committee, AGM, EGM, and creditors' meeting. A meeting records its type,
title, date/time, duration, venue or video link, agenda items (with presenter and
duration), chairperson, whether quorum was met, and the draft minutes.

**Flow:** schedule the meeting and set the agenda → circulate the agenda → hold the
meeting (mark in progress, then completed) → draft and finalise the minutes → archive.

## Board resolutions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft** | Being prepared. |
| **Passed** | Approved by the board, with the voting record stored. |

Resolution types are **Ordinary**, **Special** (requires a 75% majority), **Board**,
and **Circular** (passed without a meeting). A passed resolution stores the votes for,
against, and abstaining, plus the date passed.

## MCA / ROC filings

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Upcoming** | Due date in the future. |
| **Filed** | Submitted to the regulator. |
| **Approved** | Accepted by the regulator. |
| **Rejected** | Returned; needs resubmission. |
| **Overdue** | Deadline missed; penalty may apply. |
| **Complied** | Filed and accepted; compliance complete. |

Each filing records the form number, title, authority (MCA / ROC), category, due date,
financial year, fees, assigned owner, notes, and attachments. Common India filings
include DIR-3, MBP-1, and AOC-4.

## Share capital & ESOP

Track share classes (common, preferred, founder, and the ESOP pool), shares issued,
face value, holders, and the transfer log. The **ESOP** view tracks grants and their
lifecycle events — grant, vest, exercise, forfeit, and cancel — along with vesting
schedules. Employees can see their grants, vesting status, and exercise options.

## Statutory registers & compliance calendar

- **Statutory registers** — the registers a company is required to maintain
  (members, directors, charges, etc.).
- **Compliance calendar** — auto-generated obligations based on the incorporation date
  and financial year, with reminders ahead of each due date. This is the same calendar
  the MCA/ROC filings feed into.
