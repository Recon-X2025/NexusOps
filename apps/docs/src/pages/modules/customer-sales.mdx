# Customer & Sales

The Customer & Sales area brings together customer service, CRM, the service catalog,
and surveys. It lives under **Customer & Sales** in the sidebar.

## Customer service (CSM)

Customer Success Management (`/app/csm`) handles customer-facing cases, each with its
own detail page. CSM cases follow the same lifecycle conventions as IT tickets but are
oriented toward customer accounts and success outcomes.

## CRM & sales

The CRM (`/app/crm`) tracks accounts and deals:

- **Accounts** — customer organisations, each with a detail page
  (`/app/crm/accounts/[id]`).
- **Deals** — sales opportunities, each with a detail page (`/app/crm/deals/[id]`)
  showing stage, value, and activity.

## Service catalog

The Service Catalog (`/app/catalog`) is where users request standard, pre-approved
services. It has three tabs:

- **Browse** — the catalog of available services.
- **My Requests** — the requests the user has submitted.
- **Catalog Admin** — for administrators to define catalog items and their fulfilment
  workflows.

Catalog requests flow into the same fulfilment and approval machinery as tickets and
HR cases.

## Surveys

Surveys (`/app/surveys`) collect feedback — for example CSAT after a ticket is
resolved. Respondents complete a survey via a tokenised public link
(`/survey/[token]`) without needing to log in, and results feed satisfaction metrics
across the platform.
