# Performance Management

The Performance module runs review cycles, goals/OKRs, and team overviews. It lives
under **People & Workplace → Performance** (`/app/performance`) with tabs for Review
Cycles, My Reviews, Goals & OKRs, and Team Overview.

## Review cycles

A review cycle defines the period (e.g. half-yearly or annual), the participants, and
the review template. HR opens a cycle, employees and managers complete their reviews
within it, and HR closes the cycle once all reviews are submitted.

## My reviews

Each employee sees the reviews assigned to them — self-assessments to complete and,
for managers, the reviews they owe their direct reports. Reviews combine ratings
against competencies with free-text feedback.

## Goals & OKRs

Objectives and Key Results are set at the individual and team level and tracked
through the cycle. Goals defined here are shared with the **OKRs & Goals** screen in
the HR module, so progress stays consistent across both views.

## Team overview

Managers use the Team Overview tab to see the status of every review and goal across
their team — who has submitted, who is pending, and how their reports are tracking
against objectives.
