# Vendors & Contracts

The Vendors module is the supplier register and contract tracker. It lives under
**Finance & Procurement → Vendors** (`/app/vendors`) with two tabs: **Vendors** and
**Contracts**.

## The vendor register

The Vendors tab is a table of suppliers with their key metrics:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name / category** | Supplier name and what they provide. |
| **Tier** | Strategic, Critical, Preferred, or Managed. |
| **Annual spend** | Total spend with this vendor. |
| **Contracts** | Number of active contracts. |
| **Open issues** | Outstanding issues against the vendor. |
| **SLA score** | Service-level performance (green ≥ 90%, amber 70–89%, red < 70%). |
| **CSAT** | Satisfaction rating, shown as stars. |
| **Status** | Active, Under review, At risk, or Inactive. |
| **Renewal** | Next contract renewal date. |

The top of the page shows KPI cards: total spend, active vendors, vendors at risk / under
review, and contracts expiring in the next 90 days.

## Adding vendors

- **Single vendor** — click **Add Vendor** and fill in the form: name, type, GSTIN,
  PAN, email, phone, address, state, and payment terms.
- **Bulk import** — use the CSV import to map columns to vendor fields (name, type,
  GSTIN, PAN, email, phone, address, state, payment terms) and load many vendors at
  once.

## Vendor detail

Click any vendor row to open its detail page, where you can review and edit the
vendor's information and track its contracts, SLA performance, and open issues over
time.

## Contracts

The Contracts tab lists agreements with: contract ID, vendor, type, value, start and
end dates, auto-renew flag, and status. Use it to keep ahead of renewals — contracts
expiring within 90 days surface in the KPI card on the Vendors tab.

For deeper contract authoring and clause tracking, see the dedicated Contract
Management area under **Finance & Procurement → Contract Management**
(`/app/contracts`).
