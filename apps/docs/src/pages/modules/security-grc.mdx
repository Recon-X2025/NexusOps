# Security & Compliance (GRC)

The Security & Compliance area covers security operations, governance/risk/compliance
(GRC), ESG reporting, and the approvals queue. It lives under **Security & Compliance**
in the sidebar.

## Security operations

Security incidents are tracked at `/app/security`, with a detail page per incident.
Open security incidents surface as a red badge in the sidebar so they are never
missed. Incidents follow the same investigate → contain → resolve flow as IT tickets,
with a security-specific lens.

## Risk management

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Identified** | New risk registered. |
| **Assessed** | Likelihood × impact scored. |
| **Mitigated** | A control is in place. |
| **Accepted** | Risk accepted by leadership. |
| **Monitored** | Tracked on an ongoing basis. |

Risks are categorised as operational, financial, strategic, compliance, technology, or
reputational. The risk score is:

```
risk score = likelihood (1–5) × impact (1–5)   →   range 1–25
```

As a guide: **15+** is high, **6–14** is medium, **1–5** is low. Each risk records a
treatment (accept, mitigate, transfer, or avoid), a mitigation plan, and an owner.

## Policies

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft** | In preparation. |
| **Published** | Approved and distributed. |
| **Archived** | Superseded or retired. |

## Compliance & audits

- **Compliance calendar** — recurring obligations (audits, filings, assessments) with
  reminders ahead of each due date.
- **Audit plans** — scope, dates, auditor, findings, and remediation tracking.
- **Control evidence** — attach evidence files to controls for SOC 2 / GDPR audit
  trails.

## Vendor risk

Vendors are risk-tiered (Critical, High, Medium, Low) and assessed via questionnaires
that move through Pending → Sent → In progress → Completed. This complements the
commercial view in the [Vendors](/modules/vendors) module.

## ESG reporting

The ESG view (`/app/esg`) captures environmental, social, and governance metrics for
sustainability and disclosure reporting.

## Approvals & workflow

The **Approvals** queue (`/app/approvals`) and the **Flow Designer** (`/app/flows`)
power cross-module approval chains. See [Approvals & Workflow](/modules/approvals).
