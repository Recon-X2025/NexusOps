# How-To: Finance & Procurement

Tasks for finance and procurement teams. Full references:
[Accounting](/modules/accounting), [Procurement](/modules/procurement),
[Vendors](/modules/vendors), [GST & TDS](/modules/india-tax).

## How do I post a journal entry?

1. Go to **Finance & Procurement → Journal Entries**
   (`/app/finance/accounting/journal`).
2. Create an entry and add at least two lines, each with a debit or credit and a
   description.
3. Make sure **total debits equal total credits** — they must match within ±0.001 or
   the entry is rejected.
4. Post the entry. The affected account balances update immediately.

## Why was my journal entry rejected?

It didn't balance. The sum of debits must equal the sum of credits within a tolerance
of **0.001**. Check for a missing or mistyped line amount.

## How do I reverse a journal entry?

Select the posted entry and create a reversal. The system creates a new entry with the
debits and credits **swapped**, marks the original as **reversed**, and posts the
reversal. You can set a custom reversal date (for example, the next month).

## How do I reconcile a bank statement?

1. Go to **Bank Reconciliation** (`/app/finance/accounting/reconciliation`).
2. **Create a statement** with the account, period, and the bank's closing balance.
3. **Import transactions** (CSV or manual).
4. Click **Suggest matches** — the system proposes links to journal entries by amount,
   date, and description.
5. **Confirm** each match, or **Ignore** a transaction booked elsewhere.
6. Once every transaction is matched or ignored, **Reconcile** to lock the statement.

## How do I generate a GST return?

From the Accounting module you can produce **GSTR-1** (outward supplies, grouped B2B
and B2C) and **GSTR-3B** (summary with outward supplies, input tax credit, and any
interest). Both export in a GST-portal-compatible format. See [GST &
TDS](/modules/india-tax) for how CGST/SGST/IGST are split.

## How do I raise a purchase request?

1. Go to **Finance & Procurement → Procurement**.
2. Create a purchase request and add line items (description, quantity, unit price).
3. Submit. Depending on the total, it is auto-approved below the threshold, or routed
   to a department head or VP Finance.

The approval route is fixed when the request is created and can't be changed
afterwards.

## Why is an invoice blocked from payment?

It failed the **three-way match**. The invoice, the purchase order, and the goods
receipt note must agree within tolerance — at the header, at the line level, and on the
received value. Open the match result to see the per-line deltas and find where the
variance is. A common cause is the invoice being tagged to a **different legal
entity** than the PO.

## How do I add vendors in bulk?

On the **Vendors** page, use the CSV import. Map your columns to the vendor fields
(name, type, GSTIN, PAN, email, phone, address, state, payment terms) and load them all
at once. To add a single vendor, use **Add Vendor** instead.

## How do I see which contracts are about to expire?

The **Vendors** tab shows a KPI card for contracts **expiring in the next 90 days**.
The **Contracts** tab lists every contract with its end date and auto-renew flag.
