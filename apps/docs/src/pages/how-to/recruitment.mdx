# How-To: Recruitment

Tasks for recruiters and hiring managers. Full reference:
[Recruitment](/modules/recruitment).

## How do I open a new role?

1. Go to **People & Workplace → Recruitment → Job Requisitions**.
2. Create a requisition with the title, department, location, work mode, employment
   type, level, number of openings, salary range, and requirements.
3. It starts as **Draft**. **Publish** it to move it to **Open** and start accepting
   applications.

## How do I move a candidate through the pipeline?

In the **Candidate Pipeline**, drag or advance the application through the stages:

```
Applied → Screening → Phone screen → Technical → Panel → HR round → Offer → Hired
```

Add a 1–5 star rating and feedback when you move the candidate; it's saved with the
stage change. You can also revert to a previous stage, **Reject** (with a reason), or
record that the candidate **Withdrew**.

## How do I schedule an interview?

From the application, schedule an interview against the relevant stage (phone screen,
technical, panel, HR round). Interviewers record their feedback and rating, which adds
to the candidate's overall score.

## How do I send an offer?

1. From the candidate's application at the **Offer** stage, create an offer with base
   salary, variable pay, joining bonus, start date, and terms.
2. It starts as **Draft**; once reviewed, **Send** it to the candidate (email /
   e-sign).
3. When the candidate **accepts**, an onboarding HR case is created automatically and
   the employee record is pre-populated.

If the offer is declined or expires, the requisition stays open for other candidates.
