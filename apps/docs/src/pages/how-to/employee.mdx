# How-To: Employee Self-Service

Everyday tasks for employees in the **Employee Portal** (`/portal`).

## How do I submit a request?

1. Go to the **Employee Portal**.
2. Click **Submit a request** (or **New request**).
3. Choose what you need — an HR case, an IT issue, equipment, and so on.
4. Fill in the details and submit. The request appears under **My Requests** with a
   status you can track.

## How do I check the status of my request?

Open **My Requests** in the portal. Each request shows a coloured status dot. Click a
request to expand its full history and any updates from the team handling it.

## How do I apply for leave?

1. Open **Leave Requests** (under HR, or via the portal).
2. Enter the start date, end date, leave type, and a reason; the form shows your
   current leave balance.
3. Submit. The request goes to your manager as **Pending**.
4. When your manager approves it, the status becomes **Approved**, your balance is
   updated, and the dates are blocked on the calendar.

You can cancel a request before — or, depending on policy, after — it is approved.

## How do I claim an expense?

1. Open **Expense Claims**.
2. Create a report and add line items: category, amount, date, merchant, and a
   receipt.
3. Click **Submit** — this locks the report and sends it for review.
4. Your manager and Finance review it against policy. Once approved and paid, the
   status moves to **Reimbursed → Paid**.

Keep receipts handy: an itemised receipt is required above the policy threshold, and
categories have limits (e.g. a daily meal cap).

## How do I see what equipment is assigned to me?

Open **My Assets** in the portal. It lists the hardware allocated to you, with model
and serial details.

## How do I search the Knowledge Base?

Use **Knowledge Base** in the portal and type your question. Relevant articles are
suggested as you type — many issues can be solved without raising a request at all.
