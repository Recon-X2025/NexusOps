# How-To: Legal & Governance

Tasks for the company secretary and governance team. Full reference:
[Secretarial](/modules/secretarial).

## How do I schedule a board meeting?

1. Go to **Legal & Governance → Secretarial → Board & Meetings**.
2. Create a meeting: set the type (board, audit committee, AGM, EGM, etc.), title,
   date/time, venue or video link, and agenda items.
3. Circulate the agenda. During the meeting, mark it **In progress**, then
   **Completed**, and record whether quorum was met.
4. Draft and finalise the minutes.

## How do I record a board resolution?

Create a resolution with its title and body, choose the type (Ordinary, Special,
Board, or Circular), and capture the vote. A **Special** resolution needs a 75%
majority. Once carried, mark it **Passed** — the votes for, against, and abstaining,
plus the date, are stored with it.

## How do I track an MCA/ROC filing?

In the **MCA/ROC Filings** tab, add the filing with its form number, authority, due
date, financial year, fees, and owner. Its status moves through **Upcoming → Filed →
Approved** (or **Rejected** / **Overdue**), ending at **Complied**. The compliance
calendar reminds you before each due date.

## How do I manage ESOP grants?

In **Share Capital & ESOP**, record a grant against the ESOP pool with its vesting
schedule. Track its lifecycle events — grant, vest, exercise, forfeit, cancel.
Employees can see their own grants, vesting status, and exercise options.

## Where do I see upcoming compliance deadlines?

The **Compliance Calendar** tab auto-generates obligations from your incorporation
date and financial year and reminds you ahead of each due date. MCA/ROC filings feed
into this same calendar.
