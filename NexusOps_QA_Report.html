✓ CONFIRMED — Bcrypt Semaphore Round 2
File: apps/api/src/lib/auth.ts · BCRYPT_CONCURRENCY=8

Root cause confirmed: semaphore works by design but BCRYPT_CONCURRENCY=8 is undersized for high-concurrency production traffic.
